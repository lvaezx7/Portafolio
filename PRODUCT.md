# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, technical recruiters, and senior ML engineers who evaluate candidates. They scan quickly: 15-30 seconds per page. They want evidence of depth, not just familiarity with buzzwords. Secondary audience: collaborators and classmates from UCU who will visit to review and reference project work.

## Product Purpose

A dark, precision-engineered portfolio that proves Luis Vaez can take ML problems from raw data to production systems. The portfolio must demonstrate a specific, traceable skill progression across 5 domains — not a flat list of projects, but a narrative of growing technical depth. Success means a visitor closing the tab with one clear takeaway: this person ships real ML work.

## Brand Personality

Precise, disciplined, ambitious. The brand voice is a technical engineer who lets the work speak — confident without decoration, specific without jargon.

## Anti-references

- Generic "AI tools" landing pages: neon gradients, floating orbs, hero-metric dashboards
- Soft pastel academic portfolio templates
- Startup SaaS pages with animated blob backgrounds
- Any portfolio where the design overwhelms the actual project work

## Design Principles

1. **Evidence over assertion.** Show the stack, the problem, the result. No vague "leveraged" or "utilized" copy.
2. **Structural clarity.** The skill progression (5 domains, 30+ projects) must be instantly scannable — not buried in decoration.
3. **Restraint with precision.** Dark base, one intentional accent color, no gradient fills, no eyebrows on every section.
4. **Interactive without being distracting.** Hover states, scroll reveals, and the neural canvas background serve the atmosphere — they don't fight for attention with the content.
5. **Information density at the right places.** The project grid is the most important surface — it should be easy to find, easy to scan, and visually consistent.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. All interactive elements keyboard-accessible. Reduced-motion preference respected via CSS media query. Text contrast ≥4.5:1 on dark background.
